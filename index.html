Questions related to data management rarely produces one universal answer. The applicable response depends on context, discipline, infrastructure, applicable regulation, contracts, and institutional policy. Please contact your data steward, legal support, or RDM team for real advice.

This tool is a comic reminder that when handling research data, we all benefit from learning the regulatory frameworks and standards that apply to research projects, because it always depends.

For educational purposes only. Real uncertainty may vary.
